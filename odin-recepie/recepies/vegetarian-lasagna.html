Vegetarian Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious vegetarian lasagna recipe that's packed with vegetables and cheese. It's a hearty, delicious dish perfect for vegetarians and vegans alike.

This recipe is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the mushrooms, onion, and garlic over medium heat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Add spinach and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and parsley.
  7. Layer noodles, vegetable sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
